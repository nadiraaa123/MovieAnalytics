## 📹 Video Demonstration Overview

This video provides a comprehensive walkthrough of the **Movie Analytics Dashboard**, a dynamic full-stack web application that seamlessly combines advanced **Data Visualization** and **Generative AI** to deliver actionable business insights from a movie rental database (`dvdrental`).

In the video, you will see a detailed demonstration of the following key areas:

### 1. The 4 Core Analytics Pillars
* **Content Performance:** Analyzing movie catalog health, tracking rental counts, identifying the most profitable movie ratings (G, PG, R, etc.), and pinpointing "Dead Stock" (films with zero rentals) to optimize inventory.
* **Genre & Audience Taste:** Visualizing revenue trends across different categories to see which genres (like Sports, Sci-Fi, and Animation) drive the highest engagement and income.
* **Rental Behavior:** Investigating customer rental duration patterns, peak rental periods, and return behaviors to maximize operational efficiency.
* **Actor & Cast Power:** Assessing star power by mapping out which specific actors and cast members generate the most rentals and revenue.

### 2. Generative AI Integration (AI Analyst)
The video highlights the integration of an intelligent **AI Analyst** (powered by Llama 3 via Groq Live). You will see how users can interact with the dashboard using natural language to:
* Ask complex analytical questions about the live database.
* Dynamically modify and update charts on the fly.
* Navigate seamlessly between different sections of the application.

### 3. Business Metrics & Predictive Insights
A quick tour of the Executive Summary showcasing high-level KPIs—such as the total revenue generated ($61K+), overall transaction volumes (16K+ rentals), active customer tracking, and time-series revenue forecasting models.
